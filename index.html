REASONS TO SEEK PYSCHOLOGY SERVICES

Sometimes coming to the decision to seek help can be difficult.You migth not feel comfortable reaching out to a pyschologist in the hospital or you worry about paying for it. And it's possible you may have some reservations about confronting problems that you've held on to for a while.

At E-Therapy, we are here to help so feel free to make use of our services. Below,we have identified some of the top reasons to seek pyschological services:

  • You are not feeling 'like yourself' lately.
  • You have become dependent on drugs or Alcohol.
  • You have gone through a major life change.
  • You have experienced a traumatic event.
  • You are having thoughts of suicide or self-harm.
  • Others have expressed concerns about you.
  • An important relationship is in trouble.
  • You have developed compulsive behaviour(s).
